✦ Features

Built into your
everyday Mac life.

Lumi reads your world in real time — no setup, no cloud, no tracking. Just you and your dog.

🎵

Music Awareness

Connects to Apple Music and Spotify. The moment a beat drops, Lumi starts dancing — matching her energy to yours.

🎯

Focus Mode Sync

When you activate Focus or Do Not Disturb, Lumi shifts into a calm, concentrated pose. She respects your flow.

🌤

Live Weather

Rain, snow, sunshine — Lumi knows. She reacts to your local weather in real time, from cozy curl-ups to sunny stretches.

📅

Calendar Check-ins

Got a meeting soon? Lumi perks up with wide eyes a few minutes before it starts, giving you a gentle, adorable heads-up.

🔋

Battery & Heat

Low battery? Fan spinning up? Lumi gets sluggish when your Mac struggles. It's a surprisingly effective reminder to plug in.

🔔

Always Native

Pure SwiftUI, transparent overlay window, menu bar extra. Zero Electron. Runs silently in the background at near-zero CPU.

✦ How It Works

Floats over
everything, quietly.

Lumi lives in a transparent overlay window — always on top, always out of the way. Click her, and she meows.

  • 👆
    Drag her anywhere on screen — she remembers her position between restarts.
  • 🔊
    Swipe or click to trigger a meow. Ten unique sounds, randomly picked.
  • 🛡
    Fully offline. No accounts, no telemetry, no internet required for most features.
  • ⚙️
    Reaction server on localhost:7893 — trigger moods from your own scripts or apps.
